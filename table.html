Blackjack Table

Welcome to the Blackjack Table! Face off against a dealer in this classic card game. Aim for a total close to 21, but don't go over! Choose to 'Hit' for another card, 'Stand' to keep your total, or 'Split' identical cards into two hands. Dive in and test your luck!

Player's Cards:

  1. A♠
  2. 10♥
  3. 7♦

Dealer's Cards:

  1. K♣
  2. 3♠
  3. Q♥

Gameplay Options:

Hit
Request another card to try and bring the total to 21 or as close as possible without exceeding 21.
Stand
Keep your current total and end your turn.
Split
If you have two cards of the same rank, divide them into two hands, each with its own bet.
